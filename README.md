# Privacy Policy for Billooo

**Last Updated:** January 25, 2025

## Introduction
Billooo is a savings tracking application that works with a physical IoT device via Bluetooth. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

## Data Collection
**We do NOT collect any personal data.**

- No user accounts required
- No registration needed
- No data sent to servers
- All data stored locally on your device

## Local Data Storage
- All savings data is stored locally using Apple SwiftData
- Data never leaves your device
- No cloud synchronization
- You have complete control over your data

## Bluetooth Usage
- Bluetooth is used only to communicate with your physical savings device
- Connection is local and direct
- No data transmitted to third parties

## Third-Party Services
- We do not use any analytics services
- We do not use any advertising networks
- We do not track user behavior

## Data Deletion
- All data is stored on your device
- Delete the app to remove all data
- No data remains on external servers

## Children's Privacy
- Our app does not knowingly collect data from children under 13
- No age verification required as no data is collected

## Changes to Privacy Policy
We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.
